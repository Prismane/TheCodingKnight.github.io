Everything In One Place

Felicity is an online shopping website that was designed to ease the inconvenience of travelling long distances to purchase items in the island. Our service provides individuals with the oppurtunity to purchase both new and used items online and have the items shipped to them for a reasonable fee. Our service also provides students with easy to access items that are neccessary for the completion of their studies. We seek to offer a vast variety of items from which our customers can select from. Our service also benefits sellers, as they would now be able to get their items to the public easier by hosting their items on Felicity.
